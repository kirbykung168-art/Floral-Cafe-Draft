## THE BIG MISSES in the previous build

The previous build was 100% rose-petal Cormorant Garamond romanticism. It missed five things that ARE the brand:

### 1. The cats and dog are the brand center
- **Champa** is the **cat owner** of the cafe — the actual Tripadvisor owner-response account writes as "Cham Pa." Quote (June 2023, cafe's own response): *"Champa, our adorable feline owner, loves meeting new people and adding that extra touch of charm to our cafe."*
- **La Lune** is the second animal (cat or dog) — IG Story Highlights show LA LUNE with a cat/dog icon
- Story Highlights are literally: LA LUNE · CHAMPA · MENU · 3rd FLOOR · 2nd FLOOR — the two pets get top billing alongside the menu and floor maps
- Real customer review (Pim Goragod, 2023): *"Remember to say hello to Chaba, the owner and yes, she is a cat. LOL"*
- Previous build had zero mention of either animal

### 2. The brand wordmark is sans-serif, not Cormorant Garamond
- The actual circular logo is a delicate **white-on-black lotus-petal "n" monogram** with "FLORAL CAFÉ at NAPASORN" set in a light grotesque-sans condensed
- The interior signage (wall, awning, counter) is the same sans
- The previous build used Cormorant Garamond italic for everything, which is the OPPOSITE of the actual brand register

### 3. The flowers are NOT roses — they're bougainvillea, jasmine wreaths, dried wheat, and Thai botanical heritage
- Grid mood-board (June 2026 IG visit):
  - Hot-pink **bougainvillea** at the shopfront (matches "LA LUNE" highlight cover)
  - **White jasmine wreath** framing a Queen Sirikit portrait with the royal quote *"ขาดทุนของฉัน คือกำไรของแผ่นดิน"* ("My loss is the nation's gain")
  - Pampas grass / **dried wheat** awning at the front door
  - Climbing vines on white-washed brick walls
  - White flowers in cloud-shape arrangements
  - Antique baroque silver picture frames
  - Vintage Thai shutters in deep teal/black
- Previous build pushed rose + blush + sage — only "sage" is close to correct

### 4. The brand has a Thai royal-heritage register, not a French/European florist register
- Queen Sirikit jasmine portrait is a recurring brand image
- Vintage Thai-language interior wall text
- The "still life" compositions arrange brass candelabra, framed monarchic portraits, dried Thai grasses
- The previous "vintage florist storybook" framing was European-only

### 5. The real flower-shop has pigeon statues
- Tripadvisor reviewer (Oct 2025): *"bonus point for the statues of pigeons, beautiful"*
- Recurring interior detail — porcelain pigeons perched on shelves

## Typography (deliberate counter to previous build)

**No Cormorant Garamond. No EB Garamond. No Inter as primary anything.**

- **Display:** **Fraunces** (variable; using `opsz` 144 + `SOFT` 100 + `wght` 400/700) — modern editorial wedge-serif, totally different register from Cormorant; warm but contemporary. Lhong Tou used Cormorant Upright — this is the opposite axis.
- **Body:** **Lora** (variable) — warm humanist serif at 18px, optimal for editorial prose. Different from EB Garamond.
- **Mono / specimen labels:** **DM Mono** — used for the herbarium-specimen labels and the florist-invoice menu. (NOT JetBrains Mono — that's Akirart's signature.)
- **Thai:** **IBM Plex Sans Thai** at 400/500 — modern Thai sans. NOT Noto Serif Thai (that was previous build). NOT Bai Jamjuree (that's Thongyoy).

## Signature moment (locked direction)

**The whole site is a digital herbarium / pressed-flower scrapbook.**

Every photo is mounted on a cream "specimen plate" with a typewritten Linnaean-style label (handle, date, Latin/Thai botanical name where applicable). The hero is a layered pinned-specimen composition — bougainvillea sprig + jasmine wreath form + dried wheat — that responds to cursor parallax. The menu is set as a vintage florist invoice with dotted leaders. Champa-the-cat sits as a fixed corner mascot (small silhouette) that blinks slowly. Section transitions use watercolor-wash SVG masks.

This is brand-true (a florist's specimen book) and not borrowed from any other site in the queue:
- NOT Lhong Tou's Cormorant Upright + Seal Reveal + vertical bamboo menu + teak/amber/cream/stamp-red
- NOT Wallflowers' polaroid-with-washi-tape scrapbook
- NOT Bonci / Mother Roaster / Bluetamp / Holic Haus / Stockroom signatures
- The "specimen plate" framing is editorial herbarium, not a polaroid corkboard
