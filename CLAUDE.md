# FLORAL CAFE at NAPASORN — Bangkok, Pak Khlong Talat

A romantic, editorial single-page tribute to FLORAL CAFE at NAPASORN — the floral cafe set on the second floor of one of Pak Khlong Talat's most famous flower shops, three storeys of vintage-and-blossoms above the Bangkok flower market.

## Brand brief — verified

- **Name:** FLORAL CAFE at NAPASORN
- **Owner:** Mr. Phuwanart Chumsrikarin (Napasorn floristry, 20+ years at Pak Khlong Talat)
- **Concept:** Second-floor cafe above the Napasorn flower shop; designed by the owner himself, lined with vintage collection + crystal chandeliers + early-14th-century-style candelabrum + dried-flower-bunga manggar ceiling
- **Location:** 67 Chakkraphet Road, Wang Burapha Phirom, Phra Nakhon, Bangkok 10200
- **Hours:** Daily 09:00 – 19:00 (per Kirby's verified dossier — most recent source). Earlier sources disagree (Time Out 2019: 10–22; Siam2nite 2018: 10–20 closed Sundays). Treated softly.
- **Phone:** +66 99 468 4899 (The Drink Journal, 2024). Other numbers reported historically (09 3629 6369, 02-221-2039) deprecated.
- **Transit:** MRT Sanam Chai, Exit 4, ~3-min walk.

## Voice in one sentence

A storybook entrance above a 20-year flower shop — antique lamps glow against white-washed brick, rattan chairs sit under crystal chandeliers, and your latte arrives next to a Macadamia Cheesecake on a wooden saucer.

- **Very Berry Brownie**

- **Cherry Roast Tea**, **Lavender Roast Tea**

## Animations — brand-coherent (8)

1. **Petal-drift parallax** — three rose-petal SVG layers float gently in the hero corners
2. **Vine grow scroll-tied** — a hand-drawn vine traces down the page left edge as you scroll
3. **Ink-bleed wordmark reveal** — the FLORAL CAFE wordmark fades in with a watercolour bleed mask
4. **Seasonal-installation crossfade** — two hero photos (sunlit interior + floral-decor-vases) crossfade in a slow loop
5. **Polaroid lift on hover** — gallery polaroids gently lift + tilt as cursor enters
6. **Bloom-pulse on signature CTA** — primary "Get directions" CTA glows with a soft rose halo on hover
7. **Marquee receipt-tape** with italic Cormorant + rose-dot separators
8. **Hand-drawn underline draw** on nav anchors

All gated on `prefers-reduced-motion`.

## "Not boring" structural moves (6)

1. Asymmetric editorial hero — type weighted right, sunlit interior bleeds left
2. **Three-storey daydream story strip** — three vertical photo cards labelled "Ground · The Florist", "Second · The Cafe", "Third · The Daydream"
3. Huge wordmark with floral-vase photo bleeding through via `background-clip:text`
4. Hand-arranged polaroid gallery (random -4° to +4° rotation)
5. Editorial drop caps on manifesto + section opens
6. Manifesto / love-note on the flower market + Napasorn florist below
