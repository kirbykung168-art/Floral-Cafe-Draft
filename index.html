Plate II · The Manifesto

A florist's three-storey daydream above the flower market.

Pak Khlong Talat · since 2018

Phuwanart Chumsrikarin has tied bouquets at Napasorn for more than twenty years. In 2018 he climbed the back stairs of his own shop, looked at the empty second floor, and decided to fill it with the rest of what was on his mind — crystal chandeliers, an early-fourteenth-century-style candelabrum, framed black-and-white portraits of Thai monarchs, a dried-wheat awning at the door, and a calico cat called Champa. The third floor came later; the bougainvillea spilled in by itself.

— Reconstructed from Siam2nite (Kunn Tansuhaj, 2018), The Drink Journal (Nicholas Lin, 2024), and reviewer accounts on Tripadvisor (2020 – 2026)

the scent of flowers, the sweetness of desserts and the aroma of coffee blend into an enchanting atmosphere. — Denise, Rome · Tripadvisor · Nov 2024

You climb through the florist on the ground floor — pots of jasmine, hanging bougainvillea, a wall of orchid stems — to a landing where the wooden staircase turns. The second floor is the café proper, with the porcelain pigeons (listen for them), the cake stand, and the wide windows that look down onto Chakkraphet Road. The third floor is quieter: more cushions, more chandelier, and on a clear afternoon, the light through dried wheat looks like brass on the wall.

The cakes come from Bite Me Softly, the same bakery that has supplied Napasorn since the cafe opened. The Macadamia Cheesecake is the photograph; the Lemon Poppy Seed cake is, on the strength of a 2024 Tripadvisor review, the one to die for. The drinks are framed by edible flowers. The owner is Champa. She does not run the espresso machine, but she does inspect every guest at the door.

Plate III · The Owners

Champa & La Lune

Two pets get top billing on the IG highlights, alongside the menu and the floor maps. They are also why the café smells like fur and jasmine, all at once.
Pl. III-Aest. ≤ 2018

Champa

Felis catus calicus · "feline owner"

The calico in residence and the cafe's actual Tripadvisor administrator. Greets guests at the door, vets the bouquets, signs off on the day. Has been mistaken for a cushion exactly once and never forgave.

Champa, our adorable feline owner, loves meeting new people and adding that extra touch of charm to our cafe.
— Floral Cafe' at Napasorn · Tripadvisor reply · June 2023
Pl. III-Best. ≤ 2019

La Lune

Comes domus · "house companion"

The second resident. Whether dog or cat is up for debate (the IG highlight cover is intentionally ambiguous), but the role is clear: shadow Champa, ignore the espresso machine, claim the warmest patch of sun in the third-floor window.

Bonus point for the statues of pigeons — beautiful. (The cats agree.)
— icaro · Bologna · Tripadvisor · Oct 2025

A florist on the ground.
